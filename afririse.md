Ronami International, also known as Ronami International Limited, is a consultancy and supply of goods and services company that was established in 2013. The company operates with a focus on collaborating closely with international private companies and organizations. The primary goal of Ronami International is to assist these entities in adapting their business models to the local business environment, thereby enabling the development of effective business strategies tailored specifically for the Kenyan and East African market.

Since its inception, Ronami International has fostered collaborations with key Kenyan organizations, including the Kenya Investment Authority, Kenya National Chamber of Commerce, and Kenya Private Sector Alliance. These collaborations have led to the establishment of Memoranda of Understanding (MOUs) and joint ventures with companies from regions such as Northern Europe, North and South America, the Caribbean, and other East African companies and governments. This network of partnerships showcases Ronami’s dedication to facilitating international business relationships and promoting economic growth in the East African region.
